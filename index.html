About Me

Hi!
My names Josh, I'm currently a masters student studying Computer Science for Games at Sheffield Hallam University. Primarily I am a video game designer/developer, but after recently working at DB Cargo as part of their Tech Support and Development team, I am a confident tech support specialist with a passion for solving problems and fixing issues for people.
Last year I won 2 awards along with the rest of my team at the GameRepublic Showcase 2022 - 1st Place in Best Team and 1st Place in Best Game Tech on our game Labyrinth. You can find more information on labyrinth in the projects section below. I have been studying computer science and programming for 8 years and my interest in the field grows more and more each year! I love to create fun experiences and games for players as well as challenging opponents and experiences with my use of custom behaviour trees for enemies in my games.

I am searching for a permanent post-graduate positionin games design, development or production. You can reach me with my contact information in the 'contact' page or by following the links at the bottom!
