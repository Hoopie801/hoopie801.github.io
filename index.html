About Me

Hi!
My names Josh, currently I am the Sole Developer/Gameplay Programmer for Project Boron, an unannounced survival horror game set in Unreal Engine 5. I was the Lead Designer and Developer for Everdawn: Champions, a tactical TCG/hex battler, as well as being the Lead Designer and Developer for Billionaire's Path, a mobile auto tapper game that has reached upwards of 500k Monthly Active Users!

I received a First Class Honours in MComp Computer Science for Games, from Sheffield Hallam University. Primarily I am a video game designer/developer, but after working at DB Cargo as part of their Tech Support and Development team, I am a confident tech support specialist with a passion for solving problems and fixing issues for people.
I won 2 awards along with the rest of my team at the GameRepublic Showcase 2022 - 1st Place in Best Team and 1st Place in Best Game Tech on our game Labyrinth. You can find more information on labyrinth in the projects section below. I studied Computer Science and Programming for 9 years and my interest in the field grows more and more each year! I love to create fun experiences and games for players as well as challenging opponents and experiences with my use of custom behaviour trees for enemies in my games.

I am searching for a permanent, full or part-time position in games design, development or production. You can reach me with my contact information in the 'contact' page or by following the links at the bottom! References for all previous roles can be provided upon request!
